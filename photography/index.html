Music Photography

I started shooting concerts in 2013 when dance music was gaining momentum in India. I worked with local promoters and agencies who were bringing some of the top DJs and brand properties down to India. Over the next couple of years, I honed my skills in shooting club events in intimate spaces and extended it to outdoor concerts. I have had the pleasure of covering Deadmau5, Skrillex, Jeremy Olander, Yotto, Nora En Pure, Julian Jeweil, Pig&Dan and more, alongside a bunch of local artists in India. After a brief hiatus, I am looking to shoot more events in Seattle, WA where I am currently based. Reach out if you want to chat about your next event.
