About Me

I am currently designing multi-channel personalization experiences for customers at Victoria's Secret. Previously, I led design efforts for the Navigation team where I worked on customer-facing as well as internal projects. I also designed Wonderus, an acronym decoder app that enables teams to work smarter.

As a product designer, I enjoy working in the entire spectrum from research to design. I particularly enjoy leaning on data to create thoughtful experiences, and employing a test-and-learn strategy wherever necessary. Currently I am staying busy learning more about design strategy and designing at scale.

Over the course of the last four years, I have worked on projects in the domains of e-commerce, cybersecurity, social networks, games and healthcare. I am currently looking for a new challenging opportunity where I can create a positive impact and grow as a designer. If you are curious to know how I can contribute to your company's mission, please get in touch with me!
