About Me

"I am interested in simplifying user journeys and building quality relationships"

Hi, I am Gaurang, a Master's student at the University of Michigan, exploring the wonderful world of UX Design. I am looking for full-time opportunities beginning May 2018 or later.

Over the course of a year, I have applied human-centered design principles to projects in the domains of technology, social networks, games and healthcare. Through these projects, I have learnt how to adapt to various environments and employ my skills as a designer.

I enjoy working in a diverse environment surrounded by people with different skill sets and lots of coffee. If you are interested in having a chat, please reach out via email or any of my social media profiles!
